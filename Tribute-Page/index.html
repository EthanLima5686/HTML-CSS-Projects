Nakano Takeko

Nakano Takeko wearing her armour.

  • Early Years

    From the age of 6 to 16, Nakano experienced comprehensive and strict training in martial arts.

  • Civil War

    Nakano was worked in defense of the shogun at the time and was recognized for her ability to fight against a white weapon. Nakano was the head of an ad hoc faction of female warriors.

  • Death

    On the morning Nakano died, she engaged in battle at the Yanagi Bridge in Fukushima and killed five or six soldiers herself with her naginata before getting shot by a rifle.

  • Nakano's Wish

    Before Nakano died, she had asked her sister, Yuko, to behead her and give her an honorable burial so that the enemy doesn't use her as a trophy for victory.
